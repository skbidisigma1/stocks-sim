Getting Started with Stock Trading

Your First Steps in Stock Trading

Welcome to the exciting world of stock trading! This guide will equip you with the foundational knowledge needed to embark on your trading journey.

What is Stock Trading?

Stock trading involves buying and selling shares of publicly traded companies. Traders aim to profit from the fluctuations in stock prices.

Key Terms to Know

Stock: A share in the ownership of a company.
Brokerage: A platform that facilitates the buying and selling of stocks.
Market Order: An order to buy or sell a stock at the current market price.
Limit Order: An order to buy or sell a stock at a specified price or better.
Dividend: A portion of a company's earnings distributed to shareholders.

Basic Trading Strategies

As you gain experience, consider exploring different strategies:

Buy and Hold: Purchasing stocks and holding them for the long term.
Day Trading: Buying and selling stocks within a single trading day to capitalize on short-term market movements.
Value Investing: Looking for undervalued stocks that have the potential for long-term growth.
